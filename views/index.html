What is Partheno?

This is a javascript library you can use in any project to make pictures with patterns of your own.
Why Partheno?

Partheno has originally been thought to permit you to setup your own wooden bookmarks!

It comes from the ancient monument «The parthenon». The building used to house a large frieze and was erected through ingenious mathematical procedures.

What can I do with Partheno?

Build inlay frieze with different wood species.
You are free to adjust:

  • The number of pairs of triangles.
  • The number of columns of triangles.
  • The dimensions of the bookmark.
  • The wood specie used for the background.
  • Even and odd background wood species for triangles.
  • Mind the cutting lines with show strokes option.
  • Set if downloadable or not.
  • Set if is chamfered or not.

Use the button at the right to generate your own sample.
