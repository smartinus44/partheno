What is Partheno?

This is a Javascript library you can use in any project to make pictures with patterns of your own.

Why Partheno?

Partheno has originally been thought to permit you to setup your own wooden bookmarks!

What is the origin of this name?

It comes from the ancient monument «The parthenon». The building used to house a large frieze and was erected through ingenious mathematical procedures.

What can I do with Partheno?

Build inlay frieze with different wood species.

What are the uses?

This library could help you to make digital art

You can randomly generate glitched patterns based upon triangle shapes.

A tool helping you for marquetry work

Add your own patterns to generate a specific assembly.

Imagine bases of your own tapestry

This library has been thought like a frieze strips builder.

Imagine geometric structure

You can visualize how geometrical shapes and colors work together with your own patterns.
